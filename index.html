Doctor Sawit AI

Multi-channel agronomy chatbot · PT. Digital Sawit Pro

role: AI & Data Analyst Intern period: Jan 2026 – present channels: Telegram · WhatsApp · Lark · Android
the problem

Smallholder plantation farmers needed fast, reliable agronomy answers reachable from the channels they already use — not a separate app to learn. The chatbot logic also needed to be structured and repeatable enough to deploy consistently across very different messaging platforms, and every answer needed to be traceable back to a real agronomy source rather than the model improvising.

technical approach

The assistant is built as a retrieval-grounded system rather than a raw prompt-and-hope chatbot: incoming questions are matched against a structured agronomy knowledge base before the language model drafts an answer, so responses stay anchored to source material instead of the model's own guesses. I structured that knowledge base myself — organizing agronomy Q&A pairs by crop-health topic — and designed the conversation workflow and orchestration logic in Dify, including how the assistant decides when to answer directly versus escalate to a human agronomist.

Lung Disease Detection with SHAP

Undergraduate thesis · UIN SUSKA Riau

role: Sole researcher period: 2025 data: 3,030 chest X-ray images
the problem

Deep-learning models for medical image classification often function as black boxes — accurate, but clinically risky without insight into why a prediction was made. The goal: a chest X-ray classifier accurate enough to be useful, with predictions a clinician could actually audit.


This project explores the application of Explainable AI (XAI) using SHAP to improve the interpretability of a deep learning model for chest X-ray disease classification. A VGG-16-based model was trained to classify chest X-ray images into COVID-19, Normal, and Pneumonia. SHAP visualizations were then used to highlight the image regions that contributed most to the model's predictions, providing insight into the decision-making process and increasing model transparency.
